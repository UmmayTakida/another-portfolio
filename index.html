Our Beloved Bangladesh!

Bangladesh is a southeast Asian country. It stands on the Bay of Bengal, which is on the south and the other three sides are surrounded by India. She also shares a little (271 Km.) of the southeast border with Myanmar. The population of about 140 million. Most of the people speak Bengali, which is the state language. English is seldom spoken. There are some tribal languages too. About 85% of the total population is Muslim. The rest Hindus, Christens, and Buddhists. People of all religions live here peacefully.
